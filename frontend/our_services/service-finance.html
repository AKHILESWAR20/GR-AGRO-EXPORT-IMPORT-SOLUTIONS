Trade Finance Instruments

We offer a range of trade finance solutions to protect your payments and optimize your working capital.

Letter of Credit (LC)

Secure your international transactions with documentary letters of credit that guarantee payment on fulfillment of conditions.

  • Sight LC and usance LC
  • Confirmed and unconfirmed LC
  • LC amendment and transfer
  • Fast processing with bank partners

Invoice Financing

Unlock cash tied up in your export invoices with our invoice discounting and factoring solutions.

  • Up to 90% advance on invoices
  • No collateral required
  • Fast disbursement within 48 hours
  • Recourse and non-recourse options

Bank Guarantees

Performance bonds and bank guarantees to fulfil contractual obligations and build buyer confidence.

  • Bid bonds and performance bonds
  • Advance payment guarantees
  • Retention money guarantees
  • Pan-India bank network

Forex & Payments

Competitive foreign exchange rates and secure international payment processing for all trade transactions.

  • Competitive forex rates
  • SWIFT wire transfers
  • Multi-currency accounts
  • Hedging against forex risk
